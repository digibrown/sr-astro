Welcome! This guide walks you through the basics of using SendRemit.

## 1) Create and verify your account

- Sign up with your email or identity provider.
- Complete KYC verification for higher limits.

## 2) Link a payment method

- Add a bank account or card for funding.
- Some corridors support wallet top-ups.

## 3) Check the live rate

Head over to the [Live Rates](/rates/) page and confirm the current FX.

## 4) Send your first transfer

- Enter recipient details and the amount to send.
- Review fees and delivery options before confirming.

> Tip: For developers, you can embed the rate widget on any page.
